
INMATARIAN'S SMELLY HIPSTER ARTSY JUMPER GAME

OBJECTIVE
  COLLECT COINS BECAUSE THATS ALL LIFE IS GOOD FOR

HOW TO PLAY
  USE ARROWS TO MOVE AND JUMP
  PRESS R TO RESTART A LEVEL YOU'RE A FAILURE AT
  PRESS F5 TO RESIZE THE WINDOW TO ACCOMODATE YOUR AGING VISION
  PRESS F10 TO QUIT BECAUSE YOU CAN'T HANDLE IT ANYMORE

  PRESS ENTER TO ADVANCE TEXT SCREEN
